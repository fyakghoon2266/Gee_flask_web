Ecology & Health Sustainable Platform

Ecology & Health Sustainable Platform (EHSP) generates multiple environmental parameters derived from different satellite remote sensing images through Google Earth Engine (GEE). The users only need to provide the shapfile (*.shp) of any specific study regions, which might contain administration boundary levels with unique ID column, EHSP will automatically generate either zonal statistic summaries or raster files in the study regions upon request.


More detail information about satellite images can be found on the GEE Data Catalog. Google Earth Engine dataset
